The Equity Board

The Equity Board presented by Robinhood bridges the gap and gives students the opportunity to invest in their financial futures.

We are teaching the next generation to invest

Overview

The Equity Board is an educational platform created to support high school students with building their financial literacy.

The engine of the program is an investment simulation competition that drives students to earn, save and invest their virtual dollars on a stock trading simulator that mirrors the Robinhood app.

Students are giving the opportunity to participate with a hypothetical $10,000 investment portfolio. Students in each state will compete for the best portfolio performance, with the winner of that state being awarded a scholarship comprised of stocks and bonds at the end competition.

Students will learn via online videos, educational materials and our very own Robinhood Snacks to apply what they have learned to the trading simulator.

Benefits

Who can use it?

  • Students 14-18, high school aged students
  • Stand alone use and/or in partnership with school

Curriculum

  • Equity definitions and understanding income
  • Asset diversification and Investment accounts
  • Day Trading and Margin Risks
  • Research Resources including Snacks
  • And much more!
